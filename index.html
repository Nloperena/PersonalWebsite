Who am I?

Young, ambitious, stylish and full of positivity. This fits me as well as a freshly tailored high-end suit. Animals are my favorite, I have a background as a pharmacy technician, and love eastern-asian culture and tech. I graduated from high school at the age of 16, in 2013. From there I did not know what I wanted to do. From there I aimlessly attended college hoping to find a spark in a career feild that I wanted to be in.
There were a couple of CS classes I took, but it didn't really stick. I had no inspiration in life. Finally after finding a comfortable job as a pharmacy technician, I was able to soul-search and find out what I really wanted to do. That's when I discovered coding via Youtube. Yeah it was a hard, but it inspired me to enter a coding bootcamp hosted by UCF. I've learned and grown so much that it brings joy to my everyday life. I am a junior level web-developer.
